🚧 Accident Prevention

Understanding causes and preventing road accidents

Follow Traffic Rules

Obeying traffic signals and road rules helps prevent many road accidents and keeps roads safe.

Avoid Over Speeding

High speed reduces reaction time and increases the severity of accidents.

Be Careful in Rain

Wet roads reduce tire grip. Slow down and maintain extra distance during rain.

Night Driving Safety

Use headlights properly and stay alert during night driving to avoid collisions.

Vehicle Maintenance

Regular vehicle inspection helps avoid mechanical failures that cause accidents.

AI Road Safety Systems

Artificial Intelligence can analyze accident patterns and predict high-risk situations to improve safety.
