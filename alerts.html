🚨 High Accident Risk Zones

Artificial Intelligence analyzes traffic patterns, weather conditions, and accident history to identify locations where road accidents are more likely to occur. Drivers should stay alert in these areas.

Ahmedabad – SG Highway

High traffic density and night driving accidents are common in this area.

Vadodara – Alkapuri Road

Urban traffic congestion increases collision probability.

Pune – Hinjewadi IT Park

Heavy office hour traffic leads to frequent accidents.

Nashik Highway

Overspeeding vehicles cause many highway accidents.

Mumbai – Thane Expressway

Very high traffic density during peak hours.

Surat – Ring Road

Rain and slippery roads increase accident risk.
