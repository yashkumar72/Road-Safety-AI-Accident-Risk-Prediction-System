🛡 Road Safety Tips

Follow these precautions to reduce accident risks

Wear Seatbelt

Always wear a seatbelt while driving or sitting in a car to reduce injury during accidents.

Avoid Mobile Phones

Using mobile phones while driving causes distraction and increases accident risk.

Follow Speed Limits

Driving within speed limits helps maintain control and reduces collision chances.

Wear Helmet

Motorcycle riders should always wear helmets for head protection.

Maintain Distance

Keep a safe distance from other vehicles to allow enough reaction time.

Check Vehicle Condition

Regularly check brakes, lights, and tires before driving.
